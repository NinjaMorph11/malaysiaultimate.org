About Malaysia Ultimate

Malaysia Ultimate is the national governing body for the sport of Ultimate in Malaysia, affiliated with the World Flying Disc Federation (WFDF) and the Asia Oceanic Flying Disc Federation (AOFDF).

We work to develop the sport at all levels — from school programmes to national teams competing internationally.
